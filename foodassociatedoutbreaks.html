Food-Related Outbreaks Analysis Summary

Dataset Overview

This analysis utilizes a dataset comprising food-related outbreaks recorded between 2010 and 2015. The dataset provides critical information needed to understand trends and patterns associated with these outbreaks over time.

Summary Statistics

The analysis revealed the following summary statistics:

  • Illnesses: On average, there were 23.7 illnesses per outbreak, with a maximum of 1,939 illnesses in the largest outbreak.
  • Hospitalizations: On average, there were 1.28 hospitalizations per outbreak, with a maximum of 308 hospitalizations.
  • Fatalities: The average number of fatalities per outbreak was 0.03, with the deadliest outbreak resulting in 33 fatalities.
